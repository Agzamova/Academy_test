Description

A distinctive feature of the production line «Sherry Oak» is maturation in sherry casks, with each vintage aged in different barrels. European oak sherry casks give the whiskey a rich aroma with notes of dry fruit, spices and chocolate, while American oak sherry casks add tones of lemon, coconut and toffee.
